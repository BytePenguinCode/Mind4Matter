Our Mission

At Mind4Matter, we are dedicated to enhancing the mental health of children and young adults by providing affordable resources and support, and educating youth and families. We are committed to reducing the stigma around mental health and building capacity for young people and their families to identify problems and address them. We believe that by being advocates in the community we can make a future where mental health and wellness are the norm and supported.
