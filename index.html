Who we are

Mind4Matter is a nonprofit organization run by students that is focused on reducing barriers to mental health supports and combatting stigma surrounding mental health. Our belief is that conversations surrounding mental health should be free and open, resources should be accessible to everyone, and nobody should feel alone in their battles. What started as a casual initiative among friends has turned into a movement having 10 chapters, 1,500 volunteers, and $27,000 raised in support of mental health awareness, education, and outreach. We host fundraisers, advocacy campaigns, and pay it forward programs like Mind4Kits and Mind4Cards. We want to enclose tangible resources to those who need it most. At Mind4Matter we are not only talking about mental health we are taking action. Whether that be through local stakeholders, hosting events that inspire real conversations, or giving young adults the tools to help in their communities we are determined to make a long-lasting change. We are Mind4Matter. Because mental health matters.
